Methodology Note

Data Collection: This report analyzed 17,386 tweets across 4 companies over a 7-day period (Jan 7-14, 2026). Data was fetched via XPOZ MCP using expanded queries (brand name + ticker symbol) to capture comprehensive mentions.

Sentiment Analysis: Tweets were classified using a 5-level scale (positive, leaning_positive, neutral, leaning_negative, negative) based on keyword analysis and contextual indicators. Sentiment scores (0-100) weight positive content higher with penalty for negative mentions.

Share of Voice: Calculated as each company's tweet count divided by total tweets across all analyzed companies. Tesla's 84% dominance reflects significantly higher social media presence compared to emerging EV competitors.

Limitations: Analysis based on English-language Twitter/X data only. Sentiment classification uses heuristic methods and may not capture nuanced opinions. Volume reflects social media activity, not market share or business performance.
